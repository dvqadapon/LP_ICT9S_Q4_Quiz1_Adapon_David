Sam Yang is a 29 year-old digital artist from Toronto. He currently has 1M subscribers on YouTube, where he uploads a variety of digital art related content. He makes videos about equipment, roasting artworks, challenges, advice, tutorials, and speed paints. As of November 13 2023, he has 113 videos uploaded on his channel. Each of his videos have thousands of viewsand he also has a Patreon and Instagram account. On Patreon, he currently has 3,760 patrons. On Instagram, he currently has 2 million followers with 514 posts. (Wikiversity)
